Why?

Goliad addresses the need for an online space for human made art by developing a search engine that exclusively indexes authentic works created by human artists. This guarantees that every image you encounter has been crafted by another human, ensuring its uniqueness and originality.

How Can I Get My Artwork Indexed?

To have your artwork indexed by our search engine, you need to create an account. Then, upload your art along with documentation of your creative process.

What Constitutes the Creation Process?

Creating art involves time and inspiration. Document your journey in whatever way feels right to you, and upload this documentation along with your artwork.

Can I Skip Documenting the Creation Process?

Absolutely! Since Goliad is in its early stages, many of the images currently featured were created before the AI art surge. If you have a substantial portfolio of work created before 2022, you are welcome to upload your earlier pieces. Please provide a brief explanation in the link section to clarify the context of your submissions.
